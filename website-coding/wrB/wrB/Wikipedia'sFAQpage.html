Website review of Wikipedia's FAQ page

Originally I wanted to do my review on eBay.com, but I couldn’t find their FAQ page. So, I decided to try Wikipedia. Low and behold I found it.

To answer the question posed in the assignment; yes it does use fragment identifiers. The page easy to navigate, simple and not to flashy. The color scheme is pleasant. It is informative and easy to use. I ran it through the validator and it came up with only 13 errors and 5 warnings. That’s impressive when you look at all the code that goes into that one webpage.
